Pickleball Rules

Basic Rules

  • Games are played to 11 points, win by 2
  • Points can only be scored by the serving team
  • The ball must be served underhand and diagonally cross-court
  • The ball must bounce once on each side before volleys are allowed

The Kitchen (Non-Volley Zone)

  • The 7-foot zone on both sides of the net is called "the kitchen"
  • You cannot volley the ball while standing in the kitchen
  • You may enter the kitchen after the ball has bounced

Double Bounce Rule

  • When the ball is served, the receiving team must let it bounce before returning
  • The serving team must then let it bounce before returning
  • After these two bounces, the ball can be volleyed or played off the bounce

Serving Rules

  • Server must keep one foot behind the baseline
  • Serve is made underhand with paddle below waist
  • Ball must land in diagonal service court
  • Only one serve attempt is allowed

Scoring

  • Points are scored only on the serve
  • In doubles, both players serve before side out (except first serve of game)
  • Server calls score as: serving team score - receiving team score - server number
